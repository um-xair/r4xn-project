Typography is the art of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and visually appealing. It includes selecting typefaces, sizes, spacing, and colors to enhance readability. Choosing the right fonts and their combinations can dramatically impact how a message is perceived. A well-chosen font pairing can create harmony, establish brand recognition, and make content more engaging.

Good typography improves comprehension and engagement, especially for books, websites, and other widely-read materials. The balance between serif and sans-serif fonts, letter spacing, and line height all play a crucial role in delivering an enjoyable reading experience.
